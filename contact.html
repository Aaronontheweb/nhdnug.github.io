Contact

To reach the North Houston .NET Users Group (NHDNUG), please use the contact methods on our Meetup

NHDNUG meets the third Thursday of every month at 5:30 PM at the offices of SmartDraw in The Woodlands, Texas. There is free pizza and drinks before every meeting.

The group was founded in January 2008 by Todd Anglin.
