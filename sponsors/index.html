Sponsors

NHDNUG, a professional non-profit organization, has sponsorship opportunities available for our monthly meetings. Membership is free so NHDNUG relies on sponsorship for support.

Benefits of Sponsorship:

  • Mention in our monthly mailings and invitations
  • Introduce your organization in front of the monthly meeting and network with members.
  • Logo/Link on our Sponsors Page
  • Logo on monthly slideshow
  • Description of your companies .NET products and/or Services

If you are interested in sponsoring NHDNUG, please email {{ site.author.email }}.
